Morinaga tea collab

In autumn of 2021 the Japanese company Morinaga began a collaboration with Gochiusa consisting of Lipton branded tea beverages packaged into tea cartons customized with the Gochiusa images on them.

This collaboration was popular on Japanese social media and people soon started getting creative with their used packaging.

Well *ahem* maybe some people got a bit too much carried away.

Would you drink Sxaro's yellow pee?

The exact reason is unknown but in any case "improper use" of the packaging eventually triggered the cancellation of this collaboration.
