*Mame

The ChiMaMe corps were a Gochiuzbeki paramilitary group founded and trained by the known right-wing extremist and member of the usagi party Tedeza Rize.

They are known to be particularly dangerous cause of their proficiency in CQC.

The original members of the group included Kafuu Chino, Jouga Maya and Natsu Megumi.

After Kafuu Chino got injured fighting in the Eastern Front the group tried to replace her unique skills by hiring new soldiers.

Many new groups emerged such as KoMaMe, new ChiMaMe and EDaMame, but none of them was up to the task and to this day ChiMaMe is still not operative.
